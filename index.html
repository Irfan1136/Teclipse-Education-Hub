About Teclipse Education Hub

At Teclipse Education Hub, we believe that high-quality tech education should be both cutting-edge and accessible.

Founded by P. Esai Yazhini and A. Mohammed Aslam. With technical support and innovation from Mohamed Irfan M, the Tech Partner, Teclipse is designed to be a premier launchpad for the next generation of developers, data scientists, and engineers.

The platform focuses on building modern digital learning solutions and advanced technology-driven education systems.

We distinguish ourselves by offering an Advanced Syllabus that is constantly updated to match the rapid shifts in the global tech industry. Whether you are a student starting from scratch or a professional looking to upskill, Teclipse provides the roadmap you need to master the digital landscape.
